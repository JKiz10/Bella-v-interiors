Meet the Designer

The Person
Behind the Vision

Hello — I'm Bella. I've spent over a decade helping Houston families and professionals create homes that are as functional as they are beautiful. My approach is rooted in the belief that great design starts with great listening.

I hold a degree in Interior Architecture and have worked on projects ranging from cozy bungalow refreshes to complete luxury penthouse builds. Every client, every project, receives my full and personal attention.

When I'm not sourcing the perfect vintage piece or obsessing over tile grout colors, you'll find me at the Houston Farmers Market or exploring the city's ever-growing art scene.
